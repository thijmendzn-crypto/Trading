# Asset Profiles

## BTC/USDT (Bitcoin)

**Exchange:** Binance
**Session:** 24/7 — most active during London (08:00–12:00 UTC) and NY (13:30–17:00 UTC)
**Typical daily ATR:** 1.5–3% in normal conditions
**Correlation:** High inverse correlation with DXY, positive with ETH (0.85+)
**Characteristics:**
- Respect for round numbers ($50k, $60k, $70k etc.)
- Monday often sets weekly direction
- Weekend volume is typically 40% lower — avoid new entries
- Strong trend following behavior in high ADX regimes

**Minimum setup score:** 70
**Preferred timeframe:** 4H for structure, 1H for entry
**Avoid:** Sundays, 30 min before/after major macro news

---

## ETH/USDT (Ethereum)

**Exchange:** Binance
**Session:** 24/7 — follows BTC with slight lag
**Typical daily ATR:** 2–4% in normal conditions
**Correlation:** BTC (0.85), SOL (0.75)
**Characteristics:**
- Higher beta than BTC — amplifies BTC moves
- Network upgrade events create volatility spikes
- Gas fee spikes can signal network congestion and selling pressure
- Often leads BTC on alt season setups

**Minimum setup score:** 72
**Preferred timeframe:** 4H for structure, 1H or 15M for entry
**Avoid:** Pre-Ethereum network event volatility

---

## SOL/USDT (Solana)

**Exchange:** Binance
**Session:** 24/7 — follows BTC/ETH with higher volatility
**Typical daily ATR:** 3–6% in normal conditions
**Correlation:** ETH (0.75), BTC (0.70)
**Characteristics:**
- Highest volatility of the three — requires wider stops
- Sensitive to Solana ecosystem news (DeFi TVL, NFT volume)
- Network outage history — check status before trading
- Larger ATR requires smaller position size for same $ risk

**Minimum setup score:** 75 (higher bar due to volatility)
**Preferred timeframe:** 4H for structure, 15M for entry
**Avoid:** Periods following network outages (unpredictable recovery)

---

## General Crypto Notes
- All three assets often move together — treat as correlated
- Maximum combined exposure: 4% portfolio risk
- If BTC dominance > 55%: focus on BTC, reduce alt exposure
- If BTC dominance < 45%: alt season — ETH and SOL setups become more independent
- Funding rate > 0.1%: overleveraged long market — be cautious on longs
- Funding rate < -0.1%: overleveraged short market — be cautious on shorts
