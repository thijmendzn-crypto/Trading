# Entry Models

## Entry Model 1: Pullback Entry (High Probability)

**When to use:** Trending market, price pulling back to value area

**Step-by-step:**
1. Confirm trend direction on 4H (ADX > 25, EMA alignment)
2. Identify key support/resistance level price is approaching (EMA, order block, FVG)
3. Drop to 15M or 1H for entry timing
4. Wait for loss of momentum (RSI divergence, decreasing volume on pullback)
5. Entry trigger: bullish/bearish engulfing candle or pin bar at level
6. Stop: below/above the entry candle wick
7. Target: previous swing high/low or next major level

**Confluence checklist:**
- [ ] 4H trend aligned
- [ ] Entry at significant level
- [ ] Volume declining on pullback
- [ ] RSI not overbought/oversold on entry timeframe
- [ ] Structure intact (no BOS against trade direction)

## Entry Model 2: Breakout Retest Entry

**When to use:** Clear level has been broken with conviction

**Step-by-step:**
1. Identify a significant horizontal level or trendline
2. Wait for breakout candle with above-average volume (>1.3x)
3. Let price pull back to the broken level (now acting as opposite)
4. Look for confirmation candle on 15M or 1H
5. Entry on confirmation close
6. Stop: below/above the retest candle

**Failure mode to watch:** False breakout — if price breaks, closes back below within 2 candles, the setup is invalid. Do not enter.

## Entry Model 3: Liquidity Sweep Entry (Contrarian)

**When to use:** Price spikes to obvious stop cluster then reverses

**Step-by-step:**
1. Identify obvious swing high/low where stops cluster
2. Watch for price to breach the level with a wick or small candle
3. Look for immediate reversal within 1-2 candles
4. Entry on the close of the reversal candle
5. Stop: beyond the wick that swept liquidity

**Warning:** This is a contrarian pattern. Only valid when:
- The sweep is against the higher-timeframe trend (mean-reversion)
- The reversal is immediate and strong (not slow drift back)

## Entry Timing Rules
- Never enter at market open (first 15 minutes) without extreme conviction
- Prefer London open (08:00–09:00 UTC) and NY open (14:30–15:30 UTC)
- Avoid entries 30 minutes before/after major news
- Best entries come after periods of consolidation, not during rapid movement
